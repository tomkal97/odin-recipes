Healthy Cookie Recipe

Back to Home

Delicious and delicate cookie with a fruity and zesty kick.

Blueberry Jam:

  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 tsp. honey
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
    • 1 tsp. coconut flour
  • dash of sea salt

Cookies:

  • 2 cups blanched almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • juice & zest of 1 small lemon
  • 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  1. Preheat the oven to 350F and lightly grease a cookie sheet.
  2. Combine the almond flour, melted coconut oil, honey, vanilla, lemon juice, lemon zest, baking powder, and salt. Stir well until a thick cookie dough forms.
  3. With your hands, form the dough into a large ball. Cover and place in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  4. While the dough is in the refrigerator, combine the blueberries, honey, lemon juice, and sea salt in a microwave-safe bowl.
  5. Place the bowl in the microwave and cook on for 2 minutes. Mash the blueberries with a fork and stir in the coconut flour. Set aside for 5 minutes to thicken.
  6. With a cookie scooper, scoop out the chilled cookie dough on to the baking sheet. Lightly press your thumb into the center of each ball of cookie dough.
  7. Fill each “thumbprint” with about a teaspoon of the homemade blueberry jam.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until edges are slightly golden-brown.
