Honey Berry Lemonade Mocktail

Back to Home

Refreshing honey berry lemonade mocktail that gets rid of the booze and invites all the anti-oxidants you need.

Ingreients List:

  • 6 whole Strawberry
  • 6 whole Blueberry
  • 6 whole Raspberry or Blackberry
  • 1½ parts Honey
  • 1½ parts Vodka or Citrus vodka or Dessert vodka
  • 3 parts Lemon juice

Preparations

  1. Muddle the honey and berries in a shaker.
  2. Add vodka and lemon juice,
  3. and then add twice as much water as lemon juice.
  4. Add ice and shake.
  5. Strain into an ice-filled tall glass.
  6. Garnish with fresh berries and a lemon wheel.
